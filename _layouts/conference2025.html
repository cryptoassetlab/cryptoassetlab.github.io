CAL2025: The 5th Crypto Asset Lab Conference

Investments, Economics, and Regulation for Bitcoin, Crypto-Assets, and CBDCs

January, 23 and 24 - Milan (Italy) and online

For general enquires please contact cal@cryptoassetlab.org, to sponsor the conference please contact sponsorships@cryptoassetlab.org.

Attendance

To attend the conference (in person or remote) get your ticket at Eventbrite. Prices are:

  • PhD students: €75
  • general admission: €120 (early birds €90, late-comers €150)
